Latitude vs. Max Temperature



Observations


Based on the observation of 568 cities, as of 09/28/2018, the highest temperature has been noted in the two cities which are Fortune Foothills, US and Estacion Coahuila in Mexico. The highest hot temperature has been noted in the cities located around the tropical area. The coldest temperature has been noted in the North and South Poles, as it would be normally expected.
